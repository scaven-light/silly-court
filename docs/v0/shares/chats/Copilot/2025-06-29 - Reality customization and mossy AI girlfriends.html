On an island where objectsand the memories of themvanish mysteriously, those who remember are hunted. Its a quiet, haunting exploration of identity, loss, and the politics of forgetting.

The First Fifteen Lives of Harry August by Claire North

Harry is reborn into the same life over and over, retaining all memories. As he relives centuries, he discovers others like himand a plot to destroy time itself. Reality bends through reincarnation, memory, and moral recursion.
